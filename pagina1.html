Tema1

Soul Worker is a free to play third-person action MMORPG that pits psychic teenagers with massive soul weapons against hordes of monsters in the abandoned city of Cloudrealm. The game features detailed cell-shaded anime graphics in line with Rusty Hearts, emulating hand-drawn 2D graphics with 3D models.
